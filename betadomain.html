Beta Protein Domains

Introduction

These scripts show some examples of beta domain proteins.

Beta-Barrels

Antiparallel β-sheets are a common motif in proteins. Often the sheets are arranged in a barrel-like structure called a "beta-barrel". (Based on 1stp.pdb, Weber et al. (1989) Science 243: 85-88)

Beta-Clam

In some cases the β-sheets are arranged in a clam-like structure called a "beta-clam". (Based on 1ifb.pdb, Sacchettini et al. (1989) Proc.Natl.Acad.Sci.USA 86: 7736-7740)
