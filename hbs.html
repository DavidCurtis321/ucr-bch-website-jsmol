Deoxyhemoglobin S

Introduction

These scripts show the structure of HbS (Deoxyhemoglobin S). (Based on 1hbs.pdb, Padlan & Love (1985) J.Biol.Chem. 260: 8272-8279)

β Subunit

Here we see a β subunit of HbS with Val6 on the surface.

β Subunit Complex

Here is a complex between two β subunits of different HbS tetramers. They are held together by Val6.

Sliced View

In this view we have sliced through the complex to show how Val6 joins the two subunits together.
