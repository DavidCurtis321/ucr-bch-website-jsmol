Arc Repressor Protein

Introduction

These scripts show the changes in structure of the Arc repressor after mutating the amino acid sequence of the β-sheet.

Arc Native

This is the native Arc repressor. Note the β-sheet. See how Leu 12 points to the interior of the protein and Asn 11 points outward. (Based on 1arr.pdb, Bonvin et al. (1994) J.Mol.Biol. 236: 328-341)

Arc Switch

Residues 11 and 12 have changed order in this "switch" mutant. See how Leu (now 11) points to the interior of the protein and Asn (now 12) points outward; the β-sheet has been converted to a short α-helix. (Based on 1qtg.pdb, Cordes et al. (1999) Science 284: 325-327)
