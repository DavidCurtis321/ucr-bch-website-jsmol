Introduction

These scripts show the structure of Z-DNA. (Based on 2dcg.pdb, Wang et al. (1979) Nature 282: 680-686)


Z-DNA 1

This is Z-DNA. One chain runs 3' TO 5', while the other runs 5' TO 3' - they are antiparallel. Z-DNA is left handed and the phosphate backbone is zig-zagged. Z-DNA has a very deep minor groove and no major groove.
