ZDNA

Introduction

These scripts show the structure of Z DNA. (Based on 2dcg.pdb, Wang et al. (1979) Nature 282 : 680-686)

ZDNA 1

This is Z DNA. One chain runs 3' TO 5', while the other runs 5' TO 3' - they are antiparallel. Z DNA is left handed and the phosphate backbone is zig-zag. Z DNA has a very deep minor groove and no major groove.

ZDNA 2

Here is the top view of Z DNA. Notice the tight packing of the strands.
