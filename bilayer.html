Lipid Bilayers

Introduction

These scripts show the structure of the phospholipids in a bilayer. (Based on a molecular model of the bilayer generated by molecular dynamic calculations, Heller et al. (1993) J.Phys.Chem. 97: 8343)

Bilayer 1

Here is the arrangement of phospholipids in a bilayer. Notice the close packing of the fatty acyl chains.

Top View

Here is a view from the top. Note the close packing of the polar head groups.

Crystal Bilayer

The bilayer can exist in a highly ordered crystalline state, as shown here.

Fluid Bilayer

Or, the bilayer can exist in a more disordered fluid state, as shown here. The fluid state is more representative of the state of the phospholipids in a natural membrane.
