Introduction

This script shows the structure of A DNA. (Based on 116d.pdb, C.A. Bigman et al, J. Mol. Biol. 227 738 (1992)).

ADNA 1

This is A DNA. One chain (YELLOW) runs 3' to 5', while the other (GREEN) runs 5' to 3' - They are antiparallel. A DNA has a very deep major groove and virtually no minor groove.
