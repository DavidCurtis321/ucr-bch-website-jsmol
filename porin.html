Porins

Introduction

This script shows the structure of a porin. Porins form water channels in the outer membrane of bacteria. (Based on 1prn.pdb, Kreusch & Schulz (1994) J.Mol.Biol. 243: 891-905)

β Barrel

Porins form a 16 or 18-stranded beta barrel in the membrane with a water filled core.

Structure

The basic structure is an amphipathic beta sheet with the hydrophobic side chains pointing out into the membrane.

Porins 3

The hydrophilic side chains point inward to form the water-filled channel.
