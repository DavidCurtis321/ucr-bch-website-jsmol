Hemoglobin

Introduction

These scripts show the 1.74 Angstrom structure of human deoxyhemoglobin. (Based on 2hhb.pdb, Fermi et al. (1984) J.Mol.Biol. 175: 159-174)

Hemoglobin 1

Hemoglobin is a tetramer (ribbons) and each subunit has a bound heme group (spacefilling).

α Subunits

There are two alpha subunits.

β Subunits

And there are two beta subunits.

Hemoglobin 4

Notice how tightly the four subunits interact with each other at most of the interface. However, there is a prominent hole down the middle of the molecule. Also, only the outer edge of the heme is exposed to the solvent.
