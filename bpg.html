Introduction

This script shows the binding of 2,3 bisphosphoglycerate (bpg) to a half deoxygenated Human hemoglobin. Determine which chains are oxygenated and their relationship to bound bpg. (Based on 1b86.pdb, Richard et al. (1993) J.Mol.Biol. 233: 270-274)


BPG 1

BPG is bound between the beta chains of Hb, far removed from the two alpha -chains. Note, that both the N- and C- termini of both beta -chains lie in close proximity to BPG.
