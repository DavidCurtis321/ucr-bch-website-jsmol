BDNA

Introduction

These scripts shows the structure of B DNA. (Based on 1bna.pdb, Drew et al. (1981) Proc.Natl.Acad.Sci.USA 78: 2179-2183)

BDNA 1

This is B DNA. One chain runs 3' TO 5', while the other runs 5' TO 3' - they are antiparallel. Note the wide major goove and thin minor groove.

BDNA 2

Here is the cross section of B DNA. Note how tightly packed the core is.
